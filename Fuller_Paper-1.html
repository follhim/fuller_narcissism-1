0.1 Present Study

Given the unique position seminary students are in, we recognize the need to identify narcissism and its predictors.

The present study will examine rates of narcissism (grandiosity and vulnerability) in seminary students, and identify predictors which may help community stakeholders and clinicians make informed decisions on care and leadership appointment.

Note

The rationale above is not final, and may need revision.

We will be examining three broad categories and specific measures proposed below:

Character: Humility, Self-control, forgiveness.

Mental Distress: Depression, Anxiety, Shame.

Spirituality: Prayer, spiritual struggle, Spiritual discipline.

Note

Worth noting that sample size for T4-T6 is 365 while sample size for T5-T6 is 452. Given this substantial difference in sample size, we will conduct analyses with T5-T6.
